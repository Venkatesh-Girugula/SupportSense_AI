# CloudFlow CRM Billing, Pricing & Refund Policy

This document governs subscription rates, billing disputes, and refund policies for all CloudFlow CRM tiers.

## 1. Subscription Tiers
1. **Developer Plan**: Free. Includes 1 database connection, 1,000 API calls per month, and community-only support.
2. **Business Plan**: $49 per user/month. Includes unlimited database connections, 100,000 API calls per month, and 8-hour SLA email support.
3. **Enterprise Plan**: Custom pricing. Includes full API access, dedicated database instances, 24/7 phone support, and SOC 2 compliance access.

## 2. Refund Policy
* **14-Day Refund Window**: CloudFlow CRM offers a full refund for monthly or annual subscriptions within 14 days of initial sign-up or renewal. 
* **Eligibility**: Refunds are not provided after the 14-day window unless there is a documented system outage exceeding our SLA commitment (99.9% uptime).
* **Processing**: Approved refunds are credited back to the original payment method and take 5-10 business days to appear on statements.

## 3. Billing Disputes and Escalation
In case of charge discrepancies, customers must contact the billing desk at `billing@cloudflowcrm.com`. Any active billing dispute or refund request must be immediately escalated to a human support manager for manual review. AI agents are strictly forbidden from authorizing refunds or adjusting billing states.
